Pet fountain

Last updated: May 2023.

If your pet is outside and you want their water to stay clean (and you have an A/C or usb outlet available), you might be interested in this stainless steel pet fountain at Amazon (Price at time: $29. Sold by Ormalla)

This fountain runs very quiet as you can't hear the motor and the water does not splash.

However, this needs manual cleaning every 2 days or daily. You may want a simpler gravity-fed larger water container that can be more easily cleaned.

Fountain comes with a few filters but you can save money after a few weeks when its time for replacement and purchase Made-In-USA, cut-to-fit water filter at Amazon (Price at time: $18 for 12x72. Sold by Aquatic Experts)

Competition tested

Pioneer Pet Stainless Steel Fountain Raindrop Design: Too noisy because motor hum is audible.

Petilog Stainless Steel Cat Water Fountain, 3.2L: no auto shutoff, pump unable to reach low water

PETKIT Wireless Pump Pet Smart Water Fountain: expensive, plastic top, needs additional product to connect to wifi (wireless is only bluetooth)
